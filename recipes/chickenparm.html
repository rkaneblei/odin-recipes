Chicken Parmigana

The best chciken parmigana recipe ever!!!

This recipe is out of thie world. Simple ingrediants and fresh flavors carry this dish! It also couldnt be any easier to make. All it takes is fresh chicken, some fresh herbs, cheese and pasta! What more could you want?

Ingrediants

  • Chicken breast
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Pasta of your choice
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Canola Oil
  • Italian Seasoning, Salt, and Pepper

Steps

  1. First, pound out your chicken breasts thin. Then, coat chicken in flour, followed by egg, and then breadcrumbs
  2. Next, heat some oil in a pan and when shimmering, fry the chicken breasts until golden brown
  3. While chicken is frying, cook pasta to al dente, approx. 7 minutes
  4. Once chicken is done cooking, place in an oven safe pan and top with Sauce and cheese, and broil in the oven until cheese is bubbling and golden
  5. Serve immediately with pasta and enjoy!
